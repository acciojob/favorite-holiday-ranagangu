Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the City of Light. It offers a perfect blend of history, art, architecture, fashion, and food, making it one of the most visited holiday destinations in the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris and a must-visit landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to thousands of artworks, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – One of the most famous avenues in the world for shopping and strolling.
  5. Montmartre – A historic district known for its artistic vibe and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery, flaky pastry best enjoyed fresh in the morning.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable dish with bold Mediterranean flavors.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be enjoyed with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Baguette – A staple in French cuisine, crispy on the outside and soft inside.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate cookies with a sweet, chewy center.
