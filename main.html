Paris


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its art, culture, fashion, and gastronomy. Known as the "City of Light," it attracts millions of visitors every year with its timeless charm and romantic ambiance.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – A symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable structures in the world.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to famous works like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – An iconic Gothic cathedral with stunning architecture.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Located at the summit of Montmartre, offering panoramic city views.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A historic avenue known for shopping and celebrations.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery, flaky pastry enjoyed at breakfast.
  • Macarons – Delicate sandwich cookies with flavored fillings.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable dish with bold Mediterranean flavors.
  • Baguette – A long, crusty French bread, essential to any Parisian meal.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
